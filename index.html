About

Silicon Boom Lift is a premier provider of heavy lifting and aerial access solutions, specializing in the rental and service of high-quality boom lifts and cranes across the Bengaluru metropolitan area and the wider Karnataka region. We are dedicated to supporting construction, industrial, infrastructure, and maintenance projects by delivering safe, efficient, and reliable equipment that enables work at challenging heights and handles complex material placement.

Key Services

We provide flexible and cost-effective solutions tailored to meet the unique demands of each project, from short-term rentals to long-term leasing agreements.

Boom Lift Rental Services (Aerial Access): We offer a diverse fleet of well-maintained articulating (knuckle boom) and telescopic (straight boom) lifts. These modern units provide working heights ranging from 40 feet up to over 135 feet, suitable for both confined indoor spaces and demanding outdoor environments.

Crane Rental Services (Heavy Lifting): Our comprehensive crane services include the rental of various specialized equipment, such as mobile cranes, hydraulic cranes, and truck-mounted cranes. We provide lifting capacities up to 150 tons, designed for heavy-duty structural construction, infrastructure development, and industrial rigging projects.
