About

Silicon Boom Lift is a premier provider of heavy lifting and aerial access solutions, specializing in the rental and operation of high-quality boom lifts and cranes across Bengaluru, Karnataka, and the surrounding regions. We are committed to empowering construction, industrial, and maintenance projects with safe, efficient, and reliable equipment to reach challenging heights and handle heavy materials.

Services Offered
We provide flexible and cost-effective solutions tailored to meet the unique demands of each project, from short-term rentals to long-term leasing agreements.

Boom Lift Rental Services:
We offer a diverse fleet of well-maintained articulating (knuckle boom) and telescopic (straight boom) lifts, with working heights ranging from 40 feet to over 135 feet, suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Crane Rental Services:
Our comprehensive crane services include the rental of various types of cranes, such as mobile cranes, hydraulic cranes, and truck-mounted cranes, with lifting capacities up to 150 tons, designed for heavy-duty construction and industrial projects.
